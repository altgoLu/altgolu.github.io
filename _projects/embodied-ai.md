I am exploring the infrastructure needed to make embodied-AI research measurable and reproducible: simulation environments, task definitions, data generation, and evaluation.

My current work involves building and validating simulated robot-benchmark tasks. The goal is not merely to make an environment run, but to make it useful for comparing general-purpose agents under clear, repeatable conditions.

Some implementation details are intentionally omitted while the work is ongoing.
