Research has two kinds of output. The first is a polished result: a paper, a project page, or a released repository. The second is the trail that led there: a failed experiment, a design decision, or a technical lesson that is too small for a paper but too useful to forget.

I am keeping this site for both. It is a place to present finished work and to leave clear notes on the problems I am learning to solve in AI agents and embodied intelligence.

The goal is not to make every note definitive. It is to make my current reasoning visible, testable, and easier to improve over time.
